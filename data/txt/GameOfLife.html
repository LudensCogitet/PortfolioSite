Conway's Game of Life is just fascinating, and I'm frankly astounded that he and the others he worked with played these scenarios out without any computers at all.

This is a React app using PureCSS and Material Design icons for the menu. It represents an initial foray into React and getting a feel for its demands, possibilities, and limitations.

The simulation can become rather heavy, and I actually had to put a hard limit on the possible size of the board to avoid crashing the browser. Whether there's more performance to be squeezed out of it is a question worth asking.
